## 1. Governing Declaration

`onx-deploy` is the **Platform Foundation Runtime** — not the full ONX Civilization.

Full ONX Civilization is the complete institutional operating system for veterinary medicine, covering every business domain from clinical operations to executive governance, from HR to POS to insurance.

This document locks the full civilisation scope as the canonical reference for all subsequent build waves.

The commanding layer. Provides the Founder/C-suite with live control, approval chains, escalation handling, and strategic dashboards.

- Approval Authority Matrix
- Board Resolution Register
