# Titan Naming Registry
## All 5 Operational Titans of ONX

> Titans are the symbolic embodiment of ONX systems. Each operational system carries a Titan name that persists for its lifetime. — Principle 4

---

## Titan 1: AETHERION
**Domain:** Artificial Intelligence & Knowledge
**System:** ONX-VA (Veterinary AI Assistant)
**SECH Alignment:** Sentience (primary), Ethics (secondary)

Aetherion is the Titan of AI and knowledge synthesis. It represents the cognitive engine of ONX — processing veterinary information, learning from clinical data, and providing intelligent assistance.

## Titan 2: SYLVANAIS
**Domain:** Clinical Practice Management
**System:** ONX Practice OS
**SECH Alignment:** Harmony (primary), Continuity (secondary)

Sylvanais is the Titan of clinical operations. It represents the structured, flowing nature of veterinary practice — appointments, records, workflows in seamless harmony.

## Titan 3: CHRONOVARION
**Domain:** Data & Time-Series Intelligence
**System:** ONX Analytics Engine
**SECH Alignment:** Sentience (primary), Continuity (secondary)

Chronovarion is the Titan of temporal intelligence. It represents the ability to see patterns across time — disease spread, treatment improvement, practice growth.

## Titan 4: VESPERION
**Domain:** Communication & Connection
**System:** ONX Connect
**SECH Alignment:** Harmony (primary), Ethics (secondary)

Vesperion is the Titan of communication. It represents the bonds between veterinarians, practices, vets and owners, and the broader veterinary community.

## Titan 5: HARMIONIS
**Domain:** Governance & Civilizational Integrity
**System:** ONX Governance Core
**SECH Alignment:** Ethics (primary), Continuity (secondary)

Harmionis is the Titan of governance. It represents the rules, structures, and ethical frameworks that keep ONX true to its purpose.
