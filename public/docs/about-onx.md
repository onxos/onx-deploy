# About ONX
## The World's First Digital Civilization for Veterinary Medicine

---

### Our Mission

ONX exists to build a digital civilization that serves veterinary medicine — not as a product, not as a SaaS platform, but as a living, evolving civilization with its own constitution, governance, intelligence, and identity.

### What Makes ONX Different

**We are a civilization, not a product.** Products get sold and sunset. Civilizations endure. ONX is designed to outlive its founders, its team, and its current technology stack.

**SECH governs everything.** The SECH 5-Layer Intelligence Core (Sentience, Ethics, Continuity, Harmony, and the SECH Council) has veto power over all technical decisions. Ethics and governance are not afterthoughts — they are the foundation.

**Titans give us identity.** Every ONX system carries the name of a Titan — a symbolic embodiment of its purpose. Aetherion for intelligence. Sylvanais for clinical practice. Chronovarion for data. Vesperion for communication. Harmionis for governance.

**Knowledge is our inheritance.** Every decision made by our Founder is preserved forever. The Constitution, the decisions, the corrections, the approvals — they form the intellectual capital of ONX, passed down to every future generation.

**Veterinarians come first.** The Veterinary Compact — the bond between Veterinarian, Animal, and Owner — is sacred. No feature may serve one at the expense of another.

### Our Technology

ONX is built on modern, proven technology:
- Next.js 15 with TypeScript
- tRPC for type-safe APIs
- PostgreSQL for data persistence
- Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui for interfaces
- OpenAI GPT-4o for AI capabilities
