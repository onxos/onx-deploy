# Founder Intent Extracts
## Why ONX Exists

---

1. **"ONX exists because veterinarians deserve tools as advanced as the care they provide."**

2. **"We are building a civilization, not a product. Products get sold. Civilizations endure."**

3. **"The bond between a veterinarian and an animal is sacred. ONX must strengthen that bond."**

4. **"AI is a tool for veterinarians, not a replacement. Every AI recommendation defers to human judgment."**

5. **"Transparency is not optional. Every user deserves to know how ONX makes decisions."**

6. **"Arabic is not an afterthought. Arabic-speaking veterinarians are primary users."**

7. **"The free tier must be genuinely useful. A veterinarian with no budget should still benefit."**

8. **"SECH is the conscience of ONX. If a feature cannot pass SECH review, it does not belong."**

9. **"Knowledge is the inheritance of ONX. Every decision is preserved for future generations."**

10. **"Titans are not branding. They are the soul of each system."**

11. **"Gate 5 is the beginning, not the end. First ONX Version is complete, but civilizations grow."**

12. **"Husam builds. Kimi protects. Hadeer guides. I decide. This is the order of ONX."**

13. **"A gap is not a failure. Acknowledging what we have not yet built is maturity, not weakness."**

14. **"The Knowledge Center must tell the story of ONX."**

15. **"Veterinary medicine is local but knowledge is global."**

16. **"Data belongs to the veterinarian, not to ONX. We are stewards, not owners."**

17. **"If a feature makes a vet practice faster but less carefully, reject it."**

18. **"ONX must be beautiful. Veterinarians work in stressful environments."**

19. **"Every error is a lesson. The Correction Archive is evidence we care about accuracy."**

20. **"When I am gone, ONX must continue. The Constitution ensures ONX survives its Founder."**
