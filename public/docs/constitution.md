# ONX Internal Constitution
## The 7 Immutable Principles of ONX

> ONX is not a product. It is a digital civilization for veterinary medicine.

---

### Principle 1: Foundational Supremacy
ONX is a digital civilization first. Every decision serves the civilization before commercial interests.

### Principle 2: The Veterinary Compact
Every system must strengthen the bond between Veterinarian, Animal, and Owner. All three rise together.

### Principle 3: SECH Sovereignty
The SECH 5-Layer Intelligence Core has veto power over all technical decisions. No feature may violate SECH.

### Principle 4: Titan Continuity
Every system carries a Titan name that persists for its lifetime. Titans are not mascots — they are civilizational identity.

### Principle 5: Knowledge Immutability
All Founder decisions, corrections, and approvals are preserved forever. They may be superseded but never deleted.

### Principle 6: Civilization Transparency
ONX operates under radical transparency. No hidden agendas, no black boxes, no undisclosed data usage.

### Principle 7: Eternal Evolution
ONX is never "finished." Gate 5 delivers the First Version. The civilization continues to evolve.
